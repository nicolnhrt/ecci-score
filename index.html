Calculation Method

Weighted components

The score is a weighted blend of valuation 30%, trend 25%, sentiment 15%, macro liquidity 20%, and internal stress 10%.

Real historical base

The long history starts in 1997. HY OAS is extended with Eco3min weekly data before the recent FRED daily window, then recent JSON points take priority.
